Joshua Eckroth, PhD

I joined the Math and Computer Science Department at Stetson University in Fall 2014. I earned my Ph.D. from The Ohio State University in the areas of artificial intelligence and cognitive science, focusing on abductive reasoning and metareasoning. I concurrently serves as Chief Architect at i2k Connect, a startup company that combines machine learning with subject matter expert knowledge to make sense of large repositories of unstructured data.
